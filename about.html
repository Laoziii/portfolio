The whole class was split up into groups, and each group had a subject to work on for the project. The group that I was in had the subject of conscious creators of content and digital footprints. At first, our group of five split up the research by taking notes on each subtopic. After we’ve finished taking notes, we compiled them to be smaller, and more effective to be put into the content of our website. When the content gathering was done, we worked on the programing of the website. Three people, including me, worked on the programming of the website while the other two people worked on spelling and grammar checks. The outcome of this was a fully functional website where people can obtain information about Digital Ethics. We made this website to bring awareness to Digital Ethics because there are people out where who don’t aren’t really conscious about what they do on the Internet.
